The history about the potato

The potatoe is in latin called solanum tuberosum and is originally from South America. It was the Spaniard who brought it to Europe. At first the european didnt think much of the potatoe because there was nothing about in the Bible. But later in the 1771 the potatoe finally got what it deserved in Europe.

What is a potato?

The potatoe is known for the starch and can be used for a variety of recipes because of it. Some of these well be presented later. Potatoes grows in the ground and not in trees as some people may think. The potatoe is round and bumpy.

    • Sorts of potatoes

    We will now learn about the differents sorts of potatoes.

    • Russet potato

      • This kind of potato is the most common one. It is medium to small in size and has a ruff skin.

    • Red potatoes

      • This sort of potato you probably also have seen before. Its red (of course) but a little bit more waxy on the skin. It is more small and round and a little more sweet

    • White potatoes

      • These potatoes have a white and delicate skin. Futhermore, these are much like the russet potato.

    • Yellow potatoes

      • These kind of potatoes are more moist and waxy on the skin but also kind of velvety. Also the skin can become very crusty and the yellow potatoe is normally a bit more big than the others.

    • Purple potatoes

      • Last but not least we have the purple potatoes. These are very waxy and moist and with a firm skin. Futhermore, these kind of potatoes have a more nutty taste.
